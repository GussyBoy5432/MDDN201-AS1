WELLINGTON CONNECTIONS

Technology is growing and ever changing, but how much do we actually know about this universal connection? This website will explain Wellington's connections and how sophisticated it really is. From "stormwater", "wires", "cables" to "signs" located within the main CBD.

Router and Electricity

Router found on main Taranaki Street power pole. Internet provider for public wireless within Wellington CBD. There were several more further down Taranaki and onto Vivian. Electricity manhole found on Taranaki St. Essential for daily living and everyday use

Water and Stormwater

Stormwater pocket found on Vivian St. There are many more throughout the Wellington CBD.

Gas Meters

Public Gas meters found on Vivian St, used to measure how much power is used within the building/flat or appartment. This particular meter is a contact energy meter. This is helpful for keeping an eye on how much power is used.

Street Signs

Spray painted symbols near Taranaki St construction site. Construction workers use these as indicators for site planning.

Vivian Street

Vivian Street transformer box for nearby housing and traffic lights. A stray wire found outside the Victoria University Architecture and Design building. Usage is unknown.
